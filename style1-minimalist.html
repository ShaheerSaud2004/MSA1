About

The official Muslim Student Association of Rutgers University-New Brunswick

The Rutgers Muslim Student Association (MSA) serves as a spiritual, educational, and social hub for Muslim students at Rutgers University-New Brunswick. We strive to create an inclusive environment where students can grow in their faith, excel academically, and contribute positively to the broader community.
