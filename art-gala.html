About the Event

Celebrating creativity and community through art

The Art Gala was a spectacular evening celebrating the incredible artistic talent within our MSA community. The event featured stunning artwork submissions from both brothers and sisters, creating a beautiful showcase of creativity, expression, and Islamic values through art.

Held across multiple venues including BSC MPR, Cove, Center Hall, and the International Lounge, the Art Gala brought together our community for an unforgettable night of appreciation, inspiration, and connection.
